About Me

My name is Franz Hoerwick, and I usually go by the nickname fhoerwick online. I am a twenty year old information technology fan from the north-central USA. The primary factor that created my interest in computers and the internet was the freedom they gave me to explore and create. I still greatly appreciate both that freedom and the the ability to communicate with people of many different backgrounds, nationalities, and walks of life.
